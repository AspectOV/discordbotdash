Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how A.S.P.E.C.T (the “Bot”) collects, uses, stores, and processes information when used within Discord servers.

Information We Collect

  • Discord Identifiers: User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, Channel IDs.
  • Configuration Data: Server-specific settings and feature configurations.
  • Economy Data: User balances, progression data, and related feature data.
  • AI Chat Data: Message content submitted through AI chatbot commands.

How We Use Information

  • To provide bot functionality and slash command features.
  • To store economy balances and server configurations.
  • To generate AI responses when users invoke chatbot features.
  • To monitor and prevent abuse, misuse, or service disruption.

AI Processing (Google Gemini)

When users interact with AI chatbot features, submitted message content may be transmitted to Google Gemini for the sole purpose of generating automated responses. This occurs only when users intentionally use AI chat commands.

Message content associated with chatbot usage may be logged in a database controlled by the Bot operator for functionality, moderation, debugging, and service improvement purposes.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the Bot and its features. Economy and configuration data may persist until removed or upon server removal. AI chat logs may be retained for operational and moderation purposes.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of stored personal data by contacting: support@aspectbot.app. Please include your Discord User ID and the relevant Server ID.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect stored data. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
